Edit and launch app

Apps can only be launched and edited by their authors.

Edit the app

On the home page, click the Edit icon next to the app to open it in the builder for editing. Once the edit is done, save the app.

Launch the app

On the home page, click the Launch icon next to the app to view it in the browser. If you are in the editing mode, click Launch button lined up with the Previews and Saved.
